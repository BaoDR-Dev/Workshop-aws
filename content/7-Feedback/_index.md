Looking back on the time I spent with the program, what stays with me is not just the technical knowledge or cloud service configurations, but a deep sense of gratitude for being welcomed and given every opportunity to grow.

### Environment and People

From the friendly workspace and the patient, dedicated guidance of my mentors, to the enthusiastic support from the admin team — all of it helped a once-confused student become far more confident. My mentors never pushed ready-made answers on me; instead, they patiently let me think through problems, make mistakes, and find solutions on my own — something school textbooks rarely offer.

The environment here is genuinely positive and warm. Everyone works hard but also stays deeply connected, treating each other like family rather than just colleagues or passing interns.

### Policies and Benefits

In terms of policies and benefits, everything was handled thoughtfully and flexibly, making it easy for me to balance completing my university thesis with keeping up with real project deadlines.

### Evaluation and Suggestions

If asked whether I have any complaints or suggestions for improvement, I can honestly say that the facilities, the organization, and the care shown toward interns have already exceeded my initial expectations. At this point, I have no complaints or additional requests whatsoever.

### Advice for Fellow Students

If any of my friends ask, I would not hesitate to recommend they seek an internship opportunity here — because this is truly the best launchpad for bridging the gap between university life and a professional career.

### Closing Words

I also genuinely hope that if the opportunity arises in the future, I will continue to be part of, contribute to, and grow alongside the next chapters of this program. Wishing **FCAJ** continued brilliant growth and all the best in nurturing many more generations of talented students.
